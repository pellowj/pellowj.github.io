About Us

Phones R Us started in 2013 by John Pellow, the now store owner. John first worked from the bottom up at another phone store, but didn't like the way his manager handled the store and their customers.

In late 2012 he left that store, and started his own with the help from friends and family members, to bring you Phones R Us.

The store has had much success and since 2015, you can now order online.
